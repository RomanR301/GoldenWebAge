Your web analytics agency: Golden Web Age

As an agency from Munich specializing in web analysis, we place an explicit value on data-based procedures in all our agency's measures. We believe that without such a solid quantitative database, no decisions about product, online marketing channels or website should be made today. Therefore, part of our offer is a comprehensive web analytics and business intelligence package.

Our consultants and analysts help you organize your company in a digitally data-driven manner. Here, we focus specifically on imparting knowledge about the various metrics and the existing web analytics tools. We are also available as a competent consultant and source of supply for evaluations of access numbers, conversions, usability and funnel key figures. In order to make all data evaluable here, we are also happy to help with the concept of your web analysis measures for your responsive website.

Web analysis with Google Analytics and Matomo

Google Analytics is one of the most powerful tools nowadays, which shows you exactly how the visitors of your website behave. And the best for you: Google also offers most of its services for free. Complex setups such as multi-domains, own development and test environments, different countries, shops and ecommerce websites can also be evaluated - the problem here is much more the how than the whether. Learn more about Google Analytics 4 here

Matomo is an open source-based solution for your individual web analytics - we often use it as an alternative to Google Analytics. The user interface is also very clear, which is why it is possible to work quickly and in a structured manner with the software and to query various parameters. We develop our recommendations for action based on the data obtained by Matomo, tailored precisely to the customer.

A big advantage of Matomo is that the data is on your own server - if only for reasons of data protection. You can also take your analytics to an even higher level at any time.

Analytics tracking concept & strategy

Every website is different and therefore there are always different requirements in the area of ​​web analytics. Therefore, simply setting up your web analytics account is not enough. We at Golden Web Age are at your side as your competent contact person and develop a tracking concept that is individualized to your website and your requirements. In this, both the relevant key figures and the specific implementation time are recorded - the implementation is then carried out either by us with the Google Tag Manager or by your own IT, which we provide with the corresponding specifications.
