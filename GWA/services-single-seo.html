What is search engine optimization anyway?

The position of your own page in the search engines is directly linked to the value of a page. If the website is well placed, it will attract more customers. This ensures more sales and thus greater success for business on the Internet. However, the competition is fierce and Google in particular places high demands on those sites that want to land at the top of the search results. Search Engine Optimization, or SEO for short, are various tools and measures that are intended to help ensure that your own page is better listed on Google. These measures on your own page make it more interesting for Google and significantly more present in the relevant keywords.
